What is Slugterra?

Deep inside the Earth exists the vast underground world of Slugterra, named after the tiny slugs who live there. The Shane family are the traditional guardians of Slugterra and have kept its existence secret from the world as they train the slugs for use as living ammunition.

Eli Shane

The main protagonist of the Slugterra and the leader of Shane Gang.

Trixie

Trixie's shots are always a bullseye.She is a member of Shane Gang.

Kord Zane

A cave troll, Kord is a member of Shane Gang and a gear-head mechanic.

Pronto

Pronto is a molenoid tracker and also the first member to join Shane Gang.

Dr. Blakk

Dr. Thaddius Blakk is the main antagonist of Slugterra, and the owner of Blakk Industries.

Slugs

Infernus

Named 'Burpy', this is the signature slug of Eli Shane. One of the most powerful slugs of Slugterra.

Arachnet

This is the signature slug of Trixie. This slug throws webs and transforms into a spider when shot.

Boon Doc

The strongest healer slug of whole Slugterra. Only one of the kind owned by Eli Shane.

Flatulorhinkus

The signature slug of Pronto, leaves a very stinky odour when shot at the opponent.

Tazerling

This slug is capable of sending electric shocks down the body of the person whose being fired.
